# Decision Log

## Purpose
A running, append-only record of formal decisions made during this project, so that any decision affecting scope, design, or delivery is traceable to when it was made, by whom, and why.

## Scope
Applies to this specific project workspace only. Covers decisions from any phase of the engagement.

## Intended Usage
Add a new row per decision. Do not remove or overwrite prior rows — if a decision is later reversed, add a new row referencing the original and mark the original's status as `Superseded`. Mark unavailable content as `MISSING INFORMATION`.

**Fields / Sections:** Decision ID | Date | Decision Description | Context/Rationale | Decided By | Related MOM Entry | Impact (Scope/Design/Backlog/Other) | Status (Active/Superseded)

## Future Expansion
Will be refined based on recurring client feedback on deliverable structure.
