Business Control Rules
Control 1
An employee cannot create a new mission request if they have any existing open (non-closed) mission.
Control 2
Mission types: National (local currency) and International (foreign currency, USD/EUR based on destination).
Control 3
Long-term missions are handled as separate monthly missions — each month is its own closed cycle.
Control 4
Destination list is configurable by the Travel Team. Supports all Sudanese cities and international countries.
Section 02
User Flow
End-to-end process flow for a mission request, from creation through financial settlement.
Phase 1 — Request & Approval
1
Assigned Person creates mission request (type, destination, dates, notes)
Actor: Assigned Person
2
System validates: no open mission exists for this employee
Actor: System (auto)
3
Line Manager (Senior Manager) receives notification and approves / rejects
Actor: Line Manager
4
Division Manager receives notification and approves / rejects
Actor: Division Manager
✕
If rejected at any stage → mission archived, employee notified
Cancel rule applied
Phase 2 — Travel & Payment
5
Travel Team reviews request, enters per diem, accommodation, and transport costs
Actor: Travel Team
6
Finance GL records the amounts; HR logs employee absence
Actor: Finance / HR
7
Treasury confirms payment and disburses advance to employee
Actor: Treasury
8
Employee notified — mission becomes Active
Actor: System notification
↺
Optional: Assigned Person requests extension → re-approval cycle
Actor: Assigned Person → Managers
Phase 3 — Closure & Settlement
9
Assigned Person closes mission and uploads supporting invoices/documents
Actor: Assigned Person
10
Travel Team (Ms. Abeer) approves final amount based on submitted documents
Actor: Travel Team
11
Finance (Ms. Huda) reviews checklist: credit/debit balance, invoices, mission status
Actor: Finance
12
Finance processes settlement — balance cleared or debit noted on employee account
Actor: Finance
✓
Mission status → Completed. Reports generated.
Final state
