# Risk Management Framework

## Purpose
Defines how risks are identified, classified, and tracked across the documentation lifecycle.

## Scope
Applies to all projects listed in [10_Projects](../10_Projects/README.md).

## Intended Usage
Use as the step-by-step method during the relevant phase of an engagement.

**Key elements:** Risk identification, likelihood/impact classification, mitigation ownership, and status tracking.

## Future Expansion
Will be refined with lessons learned from completed engagements.
