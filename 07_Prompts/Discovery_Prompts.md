# Discovery Prompts

## Purpose
A library of prompts for extracting structured discovery outputs from raw meeting notes or source documents.

## Scope
Applies whenever the matching AI-assisted task is performed.

## Intended Usage
Adapt the example prompt below to the specific source material at hand.

**Example Prompt:**
> "Given the attached meeting notes, extract Business Requirements, Functional Requirements, Non-Functional Requirements, Business Rules, Risks, Assumptions, Dependencies, Open Questions, Gaps, and Suggested Next Session Questions. Do not assume any missing detail — flag it as MISSING INFORMATION."

## Future Expansion
Will be expanded with additional prompt variants as usage patterns emerge.
