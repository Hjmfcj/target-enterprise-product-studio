# Knowledge Transfer Plan

## Purpose
A ready-to-fill plan scoping what must be transferred to the receiving team (client, support, or operations) at project closure, and how.

## Scope
Applies to this specific project workspace only.

## Intended Usage
Complete each field before scheduling KT sessions. Do not remove sections — mark unavailable content as `MISSING INFORMATION`.

**Fields / Sections:** Handover Scope (systems/modules) | Receiving Team/Stakeholders | KT Sessions (Topic/Date/Owner/Audience) | Reference Materials (BRD/FRD/Backlog/Design links) | Outstanding Items at Handover | Support Model Post-Handover | Sign-off Criteria

## Future Expansion
Will be refined based on recurring client feedback on deliverable structure.
