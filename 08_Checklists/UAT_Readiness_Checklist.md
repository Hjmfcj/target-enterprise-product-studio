# UAT Readiness Checklist

## Purpose
Quality checklist to confirm a feature set is ready for User Acceptance Testing.

## Scope
Applies immediately before finalizing the matching deliverable type.

## Intended Usage
Work through every item; do not mark complete until all items are addressed or explicitly flagged as MISSING INFORMATION.

**Checklist Items:**
- All Acceptance Criteria converted into UAT scenarios
- Test environment and data confirmed
- Sign-off stakeholders identified
- Defect triage process agreed

## Future Expansion
Will be refined based on recurring QA findings across engagements.
