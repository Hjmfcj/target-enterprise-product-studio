# Knowledge Transfer Checklist

## Purpose
A ready-to-fill checklist confirming every item in the KT Plan has been delivered and understood by the receiving team before formal sign-off.

## Scope
Applies to this specific project workspace only.

## Intended Usage
Run against the completed [KT Plan](KT_Plan.md) before requesting [KT Signoff](KT_Signoff.md). Do not remove items — mark as `Not Applicable` with rationale if a plan item does not apply.

**Fields / Sections:** Checklist Item | Source Reference (BRD/FRD/Backlog/Design/RAID) | Delivered (Y/N) | Verified By | Verification Date | Notes/Gaps

## Future Expansion
Will be refined based on recurring client feedback on deliverable structure.
