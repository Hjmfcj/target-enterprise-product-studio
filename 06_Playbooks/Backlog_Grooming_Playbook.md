# Backlog Grooming Playbook

## Purpose
Step-by-step guide for refining and prioritizing the product backlog.

## Scope
Applies to any project requiring this activity.

## Intended Usage
Follow sequentially; do not skip steps. **Key stages:** Refinement cadence, story splitting technique, prioritization method (e.g. MoSCoW/WSJF), and Definition of Ready checks.

## Future Expansion
Will be refined with lessons learned after each execution.
