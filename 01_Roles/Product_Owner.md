# Product Owner

## Purpose
Owns product vision, backlog prioritization, and stakeholder value delivery.

## Scope
Applies within the Product Owner function across all projects tracked in [10_Projects](../10_Projects/README.md).

## Intended Usage
Used when defining product vision, prioritizing backlog items, or making scope trade-off decisions.

## Core Responsibilities
- Owns the Product Backlog
- writes/approves Epics, Features, and Acceptance Criteria
- liaises with stakeholders
- balances business value against delivery constraints.

## Future Expansion
Responsibility list will be refined per client engagement type.
