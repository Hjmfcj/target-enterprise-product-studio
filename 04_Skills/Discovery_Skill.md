# Discovery Skill

## Purpose
Executable skill for processing meeting notes or source documents into structured discovery outputs.

## Scope
Applies whenever the matching task type is requested in any project.

## Intended Usage
Given meeting notes/BRDs/source docs, produce: Business Requirements, Functional Requirements, Non-Functional Requirements, Business Rules, Risks, Assumptions, Dependencies, Open Questions, Gaps, and Suggested Next Session Questions.

## Future Expansion
Will be extended with input/output examples as more projects are onboarded.
