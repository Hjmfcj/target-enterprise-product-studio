# Business Requirements Document Standard

## Purpose
Defines the mandatory structure for every Business Requirements Document produced in this repository.

## Scope
Applies to all BRDs across all projects.

## Intended Usage
1. Document Information
2. Overview
3. Business Objective
4. Scope
5. Out of Scope
6. Stakeholders
7. AS-IS Process
8. TO-BE Process
9. Functional Requirements
10. Non-Functional Requirements
11. Business Rules
12. Integrations
13. Notifications
14. Reports
15. Risks
16. Assumptions
17. Dependencies
18. Acceptance Criteria
19. Process Flow
20. Appendix

## Future Expansion
Will be versioned as client-specific variations emerge; deviations must be documented, not silently applied.
