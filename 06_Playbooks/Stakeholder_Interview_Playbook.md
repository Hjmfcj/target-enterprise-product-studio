# Stakeholder Interview Playbook

## Purpose
Step-by-step guide for conducting 1:1 or small-group stakeholder interviews.

## Scope
Applies to any project requiring this activity.

## Intended Usage
Follow sequentially; do not skip steps. **Key stages:** Interview goals, question bank structure, note-taking method, and follow-up clarification loop.

## Future Expansion
Will be refined with lessons learned after each execution.
