# Gap Analysis Prompts

## Purpose
A library of prompts for running structured gap analysis against a draft deliverable.

## Scope
Applies whenever the matching AI-assisted task is performed.

## Intended Usage
Adapt the example prompt below to the specific source material at hand.

**Example Prompt:**
> "Review the attached draft deliverable against the Quality Gates in 00_System/05_Quality_Gates.md. List all missing requirements, business rules, stakeholders, validations, approval steps, integrations, and reports."

## Future Expansion
Will be expanded with additional prompt variants as usage patterns emerge.
