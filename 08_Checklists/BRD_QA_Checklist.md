# BRD QA Checklist

## Purpose
Quality checklist to run before finalizing any Business Requirements Document.

## Scope
Applies immediately before finalizing the matching deliverable type.

## Intended Usage
Work through every item; do not mark complete until all items are addressed or explicitly flagged as MISSING INFORMATION.

**Checklist Items:**
- All 20 standard sections present
- Every requirement traceable to a source
- No invented business rules or workflows
- Risks, Assumptions, Dependencies, Open Questions all populated or explicitly marked N/A
- Gap Analysis section included

## Future Expansion
Will be refined based on recurring QA findings across engagements.
