# Requirement Quality Checklist

## Purpose
Quality checklist evaluating individual requirements for clarity, completeness, consistency, traceability, and testability.

## Scope
Applies immediately before finalizing the matching deliverable type.

## Intended Usage
Work through every item; do not mark complete until all items are addressed or explicitly flagged as MISSING INFORMATION.

**Checklist Items:**
- Is the requirement unambiguous?
- Is it complete on its own?
- Does it conflict with any other requirement?
- Is its source identified?
- Can it be objectively tested?

Will be refined based on recurring QA findings across engagements.
