# Scrum Master

## Purpose
Facilitates agile ceremonies and removes delivery impediments for the team.

## Scope
Applies within the Scrum Master function across all projects tracked in [10_Projects](../10_Projects/README.md).

## Intended Usage
Used during sprint planning, retrospectives, and delivery health tracking.

## Core Responsibilities
- Facilitates sprint planning, stand-ups, reviews, and retrospectives
- tracks velocity and impediments.

## Future Expansion
Responsibility list will be refined per client engagement type.
