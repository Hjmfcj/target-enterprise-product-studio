# Minutes of Meeting Log

## Purpose
A running, append-only log of Minutes of Meeting entries for this project, using [05_Templates/MOM_Template.md](../../../05_Templates/MOM_Template.md) as the entry structure.

## Scope
Applies to this specific project workspace only. Covers every meeting held during the engagement — discovery, design, backlog grooming, UAT, handover — not only discovery-phase sessions.

## Intended Usage
Append a new dated entry per meeting; do not overwrite prior entries. Each entry follows the MOM Template structure. Do not remove sections — mark unavailable content as `MISSING INFORMATION`.

**Fields / Sections per entry:** Date/Attendees | Agenda | Discussion Summary | Decisions (link to Decision Log) | Action Items (link to Action Log) | Risks (link to RAID Log) | Open Questions | Next Steps

Will be refined based on recurring client feedback on deliverable structure.
