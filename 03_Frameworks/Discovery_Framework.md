# Discovery Framework

## Purpose
Provides a repeatable method for running discovery sessions and extracting requirements from stakeholders and source material.

## Scope
Applies to all projects listed in [10_Projects](../10_Projects/README.md).

## Intended Usage
Use as the step-by-step method during the relevant phase of an engagement.

**Key elements:** Business Objectives, Pain Points, Stakeholders, Business Rules, Approval Workflows, Risks, Dependencies, Open Questions.

## Future Expansion
Will be refined with lessons learned from completed engagements.
