# Handover

## Purpose
Stores Knowledge Transfer (KT) and formal handover artifacts produced after UAT sign-off, closing out the project lifecycle that begins at `01_Discovery`.

## Scope
Applies to this specific project workspace only.

## Intended Usage
Populate once UAT (`05_UAT`) has reached sign-off. Use the KT Plan to scope what must be transferred, the KT Checklist to track completion, and the KT Signoff to record formal acceptance by the receiving team/client.

## Contents
- [KT Plan](KT_Plan.md)
- [KT Checklist](KT_Checklist.md)
- [KT Signoff](KT_Signoff.md)

## Future Expansion
Will accumulate project-specific KT Manuals and KT decks as produced; a matching KT Manual/Deck template may be promoted to [05_Templates](../../../05_Templates/README.md) once the pattern recurs across enough projects.
