# Knowledge Transfer Signoff

## Purpose
A ready-to-fill formal sign-off record confirming the receiving team accepts the knowledge transfer as complete, closing out the project lifecycle.

## Scope
Applies to this specific project workspace only.

## Intended Usage
Complete only after the [KT Checklist](KT_Checklist.md) shows no open gaps, or open gaps are explicitly accepted with rationale. Do not remove sections — mark unavailable content as `MISSING INFORMATION`.

**Fields / Sections:** Project Name | Handover Date | Delivering Team/Signatory | Receiving Team/Signatory | Outstanding Items Accepted As-Is (if any) | Reference to Completed KT Checklist | Sign-off Statement | Date

Will be refined based on recurring client feedback on deliverable structure.
