# Business Analyst

## Purpose
Elicits, documents, and validates business and functional requirements from stakeholders and source material.

## Scope
Applies within the Business Analyst function across all projects tracked in [10_Projects](../10_Projects/README.md).

## Intended Usage
Used during discovery sessions, requirements workshops, BRD/FRD authoring, and gap analysis.

## Core Responsibilities
- Runs discovery sessions
- drafts BRD/FRD
- maintains the Requirements Traceability Matrix
- challenges unclear requirements
- flags gaps and risks.

## Future Expansion
Responsibility list will be refined per client engagement type.
