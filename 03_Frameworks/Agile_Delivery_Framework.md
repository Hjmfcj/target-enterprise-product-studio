# Agile Delivery Framework

## Purpose
Defines how validated requirements flow into an agile backlog and sprint delivery cycle.

## Scope
Applies to all projects listed in [10_Projects](../10_Projects/README.md).

## Intended Usage
Use as the step-by-step method during the relevant phase of an engagement.

**Key elements:** Backlog refinement, sprint planning, execution, review, and retrospective, aligned to the Azure DevOps standard.

## Future Expansion
Will be refined with lessons learned from completed engagements.
